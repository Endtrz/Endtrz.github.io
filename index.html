How To Use @MissMita_Bot

Get started in just a few simple steps

1

Add to Your Group

Click the "Add to Group" button or manually add @MissMita_Bot to your Telegram group.

2

Promote to Admin

Give @MissMita_Bot admin privileges with permissions to delete messages and ban users.

3

Configure Settings

Use commands like /settings to customize the bot's behavior for your group.

4

Enjoy Protection

The bot will now automatically protect your group from spam and unwanted activity.
